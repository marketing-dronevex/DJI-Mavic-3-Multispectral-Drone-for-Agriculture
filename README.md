DJI Mavic 3 Multispectral Drone for Agriculture: Advanced Crop Insights

The DJI Mavic 3 Multispectral Drone is a specialized agriculture drone designed to provide farmers and agronomists with detailed multispectral imaging for precise crop monitoring. Equipped with an integrated multispectral sensor combining a 20MP RGB camera and five narrow-band spectral sensors (Blue, Green, Red, Red Edge, and NIR), it enables the calculation of essential vegetation indices like NDVI and NDRE. The drone features a sunlight sensor for improved data calibration, RTK-level positioning for centimeter accuracy, and offers up to 43 minutes of flight time with a lightweight, foldable design for easy deployment. This system enhances decision-making for optimized yields and resource management.
